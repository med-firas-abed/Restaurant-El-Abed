Enjoy our meat

Welcome to El Abed, a classic restaurant in the heart of Tunis, known for its delicious meat specialties. Our menu is a tribute to traditional recipes and cooking methods that bring out the authentic flavors of our region. From tasty kebabs to savory stews, each dish at El Abed reflects our commitment to timeless culinary traditions. Join us for a dining experience where simplicity meets deliciousness, and every bite tells a story of tradition and warmth.

Slow-roasted lamb

Our slow-roasted lamb is slow-cooked to perfection. We take the time to marinate the meat in a harmonious blend of herbs and spices, allowing the flavors to infuse and elevate the overall experience.

Mosli lamb's head

Introducing our signature dish, Mosli Lamb's Head. Delight in the rich and authentic flavors of this time-honored delicacy. It is not just a dish; it's a celebration of culinary expertise and heritage.
